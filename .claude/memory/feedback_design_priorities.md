Web-first design, NOT mobile-first. Coaches and admins are the primary users and they use desktop.
Mobile compatibility is nice-to-have but not the driving design principle.

**Why:** The thesis mentions mobile-first but Igor corrected this — the actual user workflow is desktop-centric for admin/coach tasks.

**How to apply:** Design layouts for desktop first (sidebar always visible, tables with full columns), then ensure they degrade gracefully on mobile.
